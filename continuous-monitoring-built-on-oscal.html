Full Stack Support

Rego supports the full OSCAL schema, including catalogs, profiles, security plans, components, SAP/SAR, and POA&Ms. Built from the ground up on OSCAL, no platform offers better support for compliance as code.

Imports

Rego can easily ingest OSCAL System Security Plans (SSP) to allow for rapid onboarding of customer data. In addition, Rego can import Word versions of SSPs and convert them into OSCAL on demand.
