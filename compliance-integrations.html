Get Self-Updating Documents in the Right Format

FEATURED

RegScale supports self-updating paperwork with the ability to auto-generate Microsoft Word and Excel documents in the exact formats expected by your regulators, board of directors, or other stakeholders.

Cloud Integrations

FEATURED

We provide full support for cloud-native security hubs available in the most popular cloud hyper-scalers, including Microsoft Azure, Amazon Web Services (AWS), and Google Cloud.

DevOps & CI/CD Integrations

FEATURED

Leverage our modern, extensible platform within your CI/CD pipelines to store your Software Bill of Materials (SBOM) on every build. Open new issues based on static or dynamic code analysis and integrate vulnerability data from container scans and other security and DevOps tools.

Scanner Integrations

FEATURED

Auto generate issues based on vulnerabilities, detect asset misconfiguration (i.e. CIS Benchmarks or STIGs), and update your asset inventory on demand. We support leading industry scanners including Tenable, Qualys, and Wiz.

SOC, DevOps, & ITIL Integrations

FEATURED

As new issues are found in your environment, RegScale can auto-generate tickets in your ITIL tool (ServiceNow, Jira, or Salesforce). It can then simplify vulnerability management workflows by tying the scan results to the tickets that remediate them and tracking issues through resolution.

GRC/IRM integrations

FEATURED

If you need to output data from RegScale to a GRC or IRM system of record, RegScale's robust APIs and export options make integrating legacy tools simple to support.
