What is RegML?

RegML is RegO's AI compliance engine—trained on 10M+ compliance artifacts from real-world audits, assessments, and regulatory filings. It understands your controls, learns your environment, and predicts compliance risk before it becomes a finding.

Rather than building separate tools for each compliance task, RegML powers your entire compliance workflow: auto-mapping controls, classifying evidence, predicting risks, and answering compliance questions in plain English.

1. Understands Your Controls

RegML analyzes your control frameworks and maps relationships between controls automatically.

2. Learns Your Environment

The AI model adapts to your infrastructure, policies, and compliance posture over time.

3. Predicts Compliance Risk

Forecast control failures 30 days in advance with confidence scores and remediation guidance.
