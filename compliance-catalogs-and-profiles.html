What is an OSCAL Catalog?

An OSCAL catalog is the authoritative source of truth—a complete, machine-readable library of controls from standards bodies like NIST, ISO, and CIS. RegO manages dozens of official catalogs, all updated in real-time as new control versions are released.

Catalogs are the foundation. Profiles build on them. Overlays personalize them. Together, they create a complete compliance infrastructure that's both human and machine-readable.
