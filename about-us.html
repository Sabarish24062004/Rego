Our Story

From idea to industry leader

RegO was founded by a team of compliance veterans who recognized a fundamental challenge: organizations were spending millions on compliance programs, yet compliance remained reactive and fragmented. We saw an opportunity to leverage automation and intelligence to transform the entire GRC landscape.

Starting with a vision to automate evidence collection, we've grown to become the trusted partner for 500+ enterprises managing compliance across 40+ frameworks. Our platform enables teams to shift from firefighting audits to orchestrating continuous compliance strategies.

Today, RegO powers the compliance operations of organizations in government, healthcare, finance, and technology sectors. We remain committed to our original mission: making compliance effortless.
