Frequently Asked Questions

Your data is stored in AWS data centers located in the United States (us-east-1 and us-west-2) or the European Union (eu-central-1) depending on your selection at signup. All data centers are SOC 2 certified and comply with industry standards for security and availability.

Yes, RegO is fully GDPR compliant. We provide a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) to all customers that details how we process personal data in compliance with GDPR requirements. The DPA covers all aspects of data protection and aligns with GDPR's high standards for data privacy.

You control your data retention policy. By default, we retain data for 7 years, which aligns with most compliance framework audit trail requirements. You can configure shorter retention periods if needed. Backup data may be retained for an additional period for disaster recovery purposes.

Yes, you can export your data in multiple formats: OSCAL JSON/XML, CSV, and PDF. You have full access to your data and can export it at any time without restrictions. Our platform provides built-in export functionality for easy access to your compliance data.

Upon cancellation, you have a 30-day window to export your data from the platform. After 30 days, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our systems. We also delete backup copies after an additional retention period to ensure no residual data remains.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We may share data with limited processors who support our operations (such as cloud infrastructure providers) under strict data processing agreements. We never share your compliance data without your explicit consent except as required by law.
